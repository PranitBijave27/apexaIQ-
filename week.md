## 1. What does ApexaIQ do & what industry problem does it solve?

ApexaIQ is the **asset inventory management platform** that delivers clean, trusted data, uncovers shadow IT, and empowers organizations to find, fix, and report risks before they become incidents.

### Problem solved
Organizations face fragmented and incomplete asset data spread across:
- EDR tools
- MDM platforms
- Cloud consoles
- Spreadsheets

This fragmentation prevents:
- Accurate vulnerability management
- Reliable compliance reporting
- Proper Zero Trust implementation

ApexaIQ solves this by:
- Unifying asset discovery
- Creating a single source of truth
- Computing a hygiene/risk score
- Guiding remediation actions

This reduces overall cyber risk.

## 2. IT Asset Management (ITAM)

### Definition
IT Asset Management (ITAM) is the practice of tracking and managing an organization’s IT assets throughout their lifecycle, from procurement to disposal.

### Why ITAM software is essential

**Accurate inventory**
- Required for vulnerability and patch management

**Cost optimization**
- Prevents unused licenses and wasted resources

**Risk reduction**
- Prevents rogue or obsolete assets from being exploited

**Audit & due diligence**
- Enables fast and reliable asset discovery during audits and M&A

## 4. Why ApexaIQ is Agentless

### Definition
Agentless means collecting asset data without installing software agents on each device.

Instead, it uses:
- APIs
- Network protocols
- Cloud integrations
- Remote queries

### Advantages

Faster deployment  
No software installation required  
Lower operational overhead  
Lower maintenance cost  
Works across hybrid environments
